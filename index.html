installation

    1. include fancyButtons.css to your page
    2. copy fancyButtons images to your images folder
    3. ready! you can find demo's codes under the options section.

options

4 color options are there(Just add them after "fancyButtons" class):

fancyButtons-Blue
fancyButtons-Green
fancyButtons-Orange
fancyButtons-Red

demo's codes

blue one:

<div class="fancyButtons fancyButtons-Blue">
	<div class="fancyButtonsLeft"></div>
	<div class="fancyButtonsCenter">blue!</div>
	<div class="fancyButtonsRight"></div>
</div>
